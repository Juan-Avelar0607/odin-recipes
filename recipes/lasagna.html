Classic Comfort: The Perfect Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, a timeless dish that brings layers of flavor and joy to the table. With its rich, hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, perfectly melted cheese, and tender pasta sheets, every bite is a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Whether served at a family gathering or a cozy dinner at home, lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that warms the heart and satisfies the soul.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • ½ cup water or red wine (optional, for added depth)

For the cheese filling:

  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley or fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For assembly:

  • 12 lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
