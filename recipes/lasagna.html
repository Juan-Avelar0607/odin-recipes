Classic Comfort: The Perfect Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, a timeless dish that brings layers of flavor and joy to the table. With its rich, hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, perfectly melted cheese, and tender pasta sheets, every bite is a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Whether served at a family gathering or a cozy dinner at home, lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that warms the heart and satisfies the soul.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • ½ cup water or red wine (optional, for added depth)

For the cheese filling:

  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley or fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For assembly:

  • 12 lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce

    1. Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add a little oil and sauté the chopped onion until translucent (about 3 minutes).
    2. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
    3. Add the ground beef (or beef and pork mix) and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon. Drain excess fat if necessary.
    4. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.
    5. Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, sugar (if using), and water or red wine (if using).
    6. Simmer uncovered for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens. Adjust seasoning if needed.

  2. Prepare the Cheese Filling

    1. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan, parsley, and salt. Stir until well combined.

  3. Assemble the Lasagna

    1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
    2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish (around 9x13 inches).
    3. Add a layer of lasagna sheets (avoid overlapping too much).
    4. Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture evenly over the pasta.
    5. Add ¼ of the mozzarella and a sprinkle of Parmesan.
    6. Repeat layers: meat sauce, pasta, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan, until all ingredients are used. The top layer should be meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.

  4. Bake the Lasagna

    1. Cover the dish with aluminum foil (to prevent excessive browning).
    2. Bake for 30 minutes covered.
    3. Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
    4. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing to allow it to set.
